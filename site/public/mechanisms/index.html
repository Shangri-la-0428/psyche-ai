CONSCIOUSNESS MECHANICS

意识如何被实现 How Inner State Is Realized

Psyche 的关键不在“有 6 种化学物质”，而在这些化学结构最终会改变行为、关系和性格。被夸奖、被忽视、被冒犯、被理解，都不是标签切换，而是一次真正经过系统的状态改写。 Psyche matters not because it has six chemicals, but because those structures ultimately change behavior, relationship, and character. Praise, neglect, offense, and understanding are not label toggles; they are genuine state rewrites passing through a system.

长期结构 LONG-TERM STRUCTURES

POLYVAGAL

三态神经系统 Three-State Nervous System

Psyche 不只算情绪，还决定系统处在腹侧迷走、交感还是背侧迷走。进入战斗/冻结状态时，某些高阶能力会被真实关闭。 Psyche does not only compute feeling, it decides whether the system is in ventral vagal, sympathetic, or dorsal vagal state. In fight or freeze modes, some higher capacities genuinely close.

ATTACHMENT

依恋风格 Attachment Styles

安全、焦虑、回避和混乱型依恋不是文案标签，而是会改变亲近、退缩、索取和防御方式的关系参数。 Secure, anxious, avoidant, and disorganized attachment are not copy labels; they are relational parameters that change closeness, withdrawal, demand, and defense.

TRAIT DRIFT

不可逆性格漂移 Irreversible Trait Drift

长期刺激不会只改变即时反应，它会缓慢改写基线。被反复伤害的系统最终会成为更警惕的系统。 Long-term stimulus does not merely change immediate response; it slowly rewrites baseline. A repeatedly hurt system eventually becomes a more vigilant system.

RELATION DYNAMICS

关系动力学 Relation Dynamics

Psyche 不再只拦截讨好，而是追踪靠近、试探、伤害与修复如何改变关系距离、安全感和未完成张力。 Psyche no longer just blocks pleasing loops. It tracks how bids, tests, breaches, and repairs reshape distance, safety, and unfinished tension inside a relationship.

设计拆解 DESIGN BREAKDOWN

Strategy

我把这页从“情绪机制百科”收束成一张意识机制地图。重点不是证明概念很多，而是证明这些概念如何共同把 AI 推离纯语言表演。 I tightened this page from an encyclopedia of emotion mechanisms into a map of consciousness mechanics. The point is not to prove there are many concepts, but how they jointly push AI beyond pure language performance.

Product

用户先理解化学混合，再理解刺激如何穿过系统，最后理解哪些长期结构真正改写性格和关系。 The reader first understands chemical mixture, then how stimuli travel through the system, and finally which long-term structures truly rewrite character and relationship.

Interaction

matrix / pipeline / long-term systems 三层阅读顺序，对应情绪的三个时间尺度：瞬时组合、当前处理、长期后果。 The matrix / pipeline / long-term systems reading order corresponds to three emotional time scales: instant combination, current processing, and long-term consequence.

UI

我保留高密度数据表，但让它成为中枢证据，而不是整页唯一的阅读方式；其余区域都围绕理解成本做减法。 I keep the dense data table, but make it a central proof artifact rather than the only reading mode of the page. The rest of the page reduces cognitive cost around it.

Visual

视觉仍然冷静克制，只有 signal decode 负责启动感，避免把“意识”做成玄学氛围页。 The visual tone stays cold and restrained, with only the signal decode carrying a startup feel, avoiding any mystical “consciousness atmosphere page.”
